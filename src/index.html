Home Purchase Using Securities Based Loan

This interactive calculator models a home purchase using a Securities-Based Loan (SBL) alongside a traditional mortgage. It allows you to adjust your home purchase price, portfolio value, SBL fraction, mortgage interest rate, property tax rate, and effective income tax. The calculator shows how your portfolio grows over time, both before and after the home purchase, while accounting for SBL interest. It also computes your monthly mortgage payments and breaks down what percentage of your after-tax income goes toward principal, interest, and property taxes, giving a clear picture of affordability and cash flow.
